WHO?

Being in love with music his whole life, Tony joined the entertainment scene in 2015, intrigued with spinning records and making crowds of people dance. In 2016 he took the decision to move from his native Buenos Aires to NYC, to be around and involved in the scene that he most loves. He is an eclectic artist who loves to spin: Deep/House, Nu Disco, Soul/Funk/Disco, Nu-Jazz, Soulful house. You can find him playing in various Hotel Rooftops around NYC. Based in SoHo, New York.
